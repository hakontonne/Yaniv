### Basic rules

The goal of the game is to have a sum of equal or less then 5 on your hand, special cards apply as such:



| **Card**    | **Value**                           |
| ------- | ------------------------------- |
| A (ace) | 1                               |
| 2 - 10  | 2 - 10                          |
| 10 <    | 10                              |
| Joker   | Chosen by the player (can be 0) |
